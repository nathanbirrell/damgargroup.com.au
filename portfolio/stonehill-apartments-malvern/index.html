Stonehill

Apartments, Malvern

On the corner of Glenferrie and Toorak Roads, within a short stroll to Kooyong Village and both Malvern and Armadale shopping precincts nearby, Stonehill is a development with six generous, three-bedroom apartments, designed by renowned Melbourne architects, Nicholas Day.

These neo gothic style apartments feature an abundance of natural light and ample space. A penthouse apartment completes the development, offering exceptional panoramic views to the city of Melbourne and its surrounding suburbs.
